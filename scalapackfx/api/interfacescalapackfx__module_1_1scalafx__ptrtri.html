private

Computes the inverse of a symmetric/Hermitian positive definite matrix.

Parameters
[in,out]aaCholesky decomposed matrix A on entry, inverse on exit.
[in]descaDescriptor of A.
[in]uploSpecifies whether lower ("L") or upper ("U") part of A contains the matrix. Default: "L".
[in]diagSpecifies whether A is unit triangular ("U") or not ("N"). Default: "N".
[in]iaFirst row of the submatrix of A. Default: 1
[in]jaFirst column of the submatrix of A. Default: 1
[in]nnNumber of columns in the submatrix of A. Default: desca(M_).
[out]infoInfo flag. If not specified and error occurs, the subroutine stops.
