Parameters

[in]descaDescriptor of A.
[in]uploSpecifies whether lower ("L") or upper ("U") part of A contains the matrix. Default: "L".
[in]iaFirst row of the submatrix of A. Default: 1
[in]jaFirst column of the submatrix of A. Default: 1
